description: "Effective bass fishing strategies as the water warms up in spring."
publishDate: 2026-01-13

---

Springtime bass are aggressive but selective. Focus on shallow areas with structure like docks, fallen trees, and weed edges. Use bright colors early in the morning and natural patterns later in the day. Slow rolling spinnerbaits and jerkbaits are particularly effective during pre-spawn.
